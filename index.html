About Our Campaign

We're on a mission to transform our community through the power of cycling.

The Cycle Together campaign aims to promote cycling as a sustainable, healthy, and enjoyable mode of transportation. We believe that by encouraging more people to cycle, we can reduce traffic congestion, improve air quality, enhance public health, and create a more connected community.
